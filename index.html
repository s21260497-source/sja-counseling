Meet Mr. Jose Routsis

College & Emotional Counselor

Personal Information

Mr. Jose Routsis has been a college and emotional counselor for over 10 years. He works at Saint Johnsbury Academy Jeju and is known for his warm personality and strong dedication to helping students achieve their academic and emotional goals.

Background

Mr. Routsis earned his Master’s degree in Counseling and Education from the University of Toronto. Before becoming a counselor, he worked as a high school teacher and later specialized in college guidance and emotional wellness. He believes every student deserves personal support to find their best path in life.

How He Helps Students

  • Guides students through the college application process
  • Helps with essay writing, resume building, and interview preparation
  • Provides emotional support and stress management strategies
  • Helps students discover their strengths and career interests
  • Encourages balance between academic success and mental health

Favorite Quote

"Success isn’t just about getting into college — it’s about finding who you are along the way."
